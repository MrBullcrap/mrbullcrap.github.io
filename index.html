🎱 8-Ball Pool
WPA Official Rules

To shoot: Touch and drag the cue away from the ball (like pulling back a real cue). Release to fire. Drag further = more power.
✕

8-Ball Rules (WPA)

Objective: Pocket all your balls (solids 1–7 or stripes 9–15) then the 8-ball.

Break: Cue ball behind head string. Must pocket a ball or drive 4+ to a rail. 8 on break: re-spot and continue.

Open table: After break, table is "open" — first legally pocketed ball assigns groups.

Fouls → Ball in hand: Scratch, wrong ball first, no rail contact after hit, miss all balls.

Win: Clear your group, then legally pocket the 8-ball.

Lose: Pocket 8 prematurely, scratch on 8, 8 off table.

Controls: Drag the cue BACKWARDS (away from the target direction). Release to shoot.
